JALEBI

Jalebi, also known as zulbia and zalabia,
is a sweet popular food in some parts of South Asia,
West Asia, North Africa, and East Africa. It is made by deep-frying
maida flour batter in pretzel or circular shapes, which are then soaked
in sugar syrup. They are particularly popular in the Indian subcontinent and Iran.
