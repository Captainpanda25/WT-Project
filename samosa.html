SAMOSA

A samosa, sambusa, sambuus, samoosa, or samboksa is a fried
or baked dish with a savoury filling, such as spiced potatoes,
onions, peas, or lentils. It may take different forms,
including triangular, cone, or half-moon shapes, depending
on the region.
