Jalebi

Jalebi, also known as zulbia and zalabia, is a sweet popular food in some parts of South Asia, West Asia, North Africa, and East Africa. It is made by deep-frying maida flour batter in pretzel or circular shapes, which are then soaked in sugar syrup. They are particularly popular in the Indian subcontinent and Iran.

Gulab Jamun

Gulab jamun are a milk-solid-based sweet from the Indian subcontinent, originating in northern India, notably popular in India, Nepal, Pakistan, and Bangladesh, as well as Myanmar.
